# ADR-0042: Team-scoring's wrong-signed covariance is a missing volume-rate→shot-count pathway, not a pace or make-value knob

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-06-03

## Context

ADR-0041 measured the team-scoring-dispersion defect as three-axis and identified
*the covariance sign* as the deficit (real `Cov(lnFGA,lnPPS) = +0.00027`, engine
`−0.00262`; artifact-free scoring-on-volume slope `Cov(lnPF,lnFGA)/Var(lnFGA)` =
1.20 real vs 0.24 engine) but deliberately did not name the generating mechanism —
that RE was scoped as the model PR's first task, measure-then-trace. This ADR
records the trace result. The full evidence is
`engine/docs/team-scoring-coupling-trace.md`; every engine claim is grep-reproducible
and the decomposition terms come from the committed #972 artifact
`engine/internal/validate/testdata/calibration-5.60-20260603-channel-decomp.json`.

The trace located the generator as a structural **absence**:

1. **The engine has no shot-volume-rate → shot-COUNT pathway.** A team's shot-volume
   rates (`r_fga/r_3ga/r_fta`) feed only shot-*type* (the 2pt bucket composite,
   `bucketweights.go`), 3pt propensity (`possession.go:32-38`), and the transition
   trigger rate (`transition.go:29-35`) — never *how many* shots the team takes.
   Half-court FGA ≈ the possession count, which is a single shared per-game step
   keyed to the **defensive** composite only (`gameloop.go:29-60`, `tempo.go:21-39`)
   and varies just ~0.8% team-to-team. So nothing scales a team's attempt *count* to
   its (positively-coupled) volume rates.

2. **The input coupling exists; the engine discards it.** Across the 28 active IBL
   teams, `corr(VOL-rate, FGP) = +0.265`, `corr(VOL-rate, O-composite) = +0.416`
   (`bin/db-query`, minutes-weighted) — teams that shoot more *are* more efficient,
   the same positive structure real teams have. Because that coupling has no channel
   to output shot count, the residual driver of output FGA variance is the
   miss→rebound→retry / turnover loop (`possession.go:73-139`: a make ends the trip
   at one FGA, a missed-then-offensive-rebounded shot adds another), which does not
   score. Output FGA variance is therefore **miss-dominated, not make-dominated**:
   only 24% converts to points (slope 0.24); **76% is "empty" FGA**
   (`Cov(lnFGA,lnPPS) = −0.76 × Var(lnFGA)`). In 5.60 FGA varies because good
   offenses take more *makeable* shots, so FGA tracks PF (slope 1.20).

3. **Two independent corroborations.** ADR-0040 candidate A (#966) routed real volume
   rates into the bucket = shot-*type* and was null on dispersion — exactly because
   it fed a type allocator, not the missing count channel. And the −23.5 ppg / −25
   FGA level deficit (`reference_jsb_season_aggregate_verdict`) shares this root: with
   no volume→count channel the engine cannot reproduce high-volume attempt totals.

## Decision

The wrong-signed covariance — the ADR-0041 deficit — is generated by the **missing
shot-volume-rate → shot-COUNT pathway**, not by a pace inversion, a fatigue effect,
or a make-value weight. The downstream model-fix PR (PR 2 of 2; this is PR 1, the RE
trace) inherits this scoped direction:

- **Build a shot-volume-rate → shot-COUNT pathway — PRIMARY, the model-PR target.**
  Make team FGA vary via coupled makes/volume rather than misses, faithful to 5.60's
  per-team `base_time` (a team **offensive/defensive** stat ratio, `00_MASTER_REFERENCE.md`
  L690-702 / FUN_004e4150, re-verified 2026-05-30 — the port kept only the defensive
  side, `tempo.go:14-20`) plus its volume-rate mechanics. Because the input coupling
  already exists and both marginals are *over*-wide (ADR-0041), the channel must
  **replace** a dispersion source, not add one: it should narrow `Var(lnFGA)` toward
  real *while* flipping the covariance sign. Optimize total `Var(lnPF)` and the
  covariance sign — **not** the slope (gameable by narrowing volume).
- **The within-possession empty-FGA source split is the fix PR's calibration item,
  not pre-decided here — BOUNDED.** Which of {offensive-rebound continuation (cap
  `maxOffensiveRebounds = 8`), TVR turnover rate, foul-draw diversion to FT} carries
  how much of the empty FGA is not linear in team ratings and needs sim
  instrumentation; that belongs to the fix PR, after the count channel exists.

The specific within-possession attribution is **deliberately not named here**: the
trace's measurement method (cross-team rating correlations) located the mechanism
*class* and refuted the alternatives, but reached its limit at the within-possession
loop. Asserting a precise within-loop lever now would repeat the ADR-0040 error of
porting a guessed mechanism.

## Alternatives Considered

- **Defense-inverted pace generates the sign** (`teamBaseTime ∝ +D` ⇒ `FGA ∝ −D`;
  positive O/D correlation ⇒ `Cov < 0`) — **Rejected, refuted by data.** Through
  PPS's dominant FGP channel `corr(D, FGP) = −0.117` (would push the sign *positive*),
  and pace varies only ~0.8% — far too small to source the 2.6×-too-wide
  `Var(lnFGA)`. Pace is a minor, defense-keyed volume term.
- **Fatigue/energy generates the sign** — **Rejected, structurally inert** (live
  energy "inert under current curve", FG-make fatigue ≈ 1.0; `possession.go:85,152`,
  `transition.go:106`).
- **Reweight `net` / tune a make-value or dispersion constant** — **Rejected,
  inherited** (ADR-0040 §4, ADR-0041): make value is net-light by design and both
  marginals are already too wide; pushing a marginal knob is the wrong axis.
- **"Add offense to the pace clamp"** — **Rejected** as the lever: pace is too minor
  a volume driver (~0.8%); the channel must reach shot *count*, not the [13,16] clamp.
- **Pre-name the within-possession empty-FGA lever now** — **Rejected**: not
  attributable by rating correlations; needs sim instrumentation (out of scope for a
  docs-only trace, no ablation rig). It is the fix PR's first calibration task.

## Consequences

- Positive: the fix PR inherits a magnitude-coherent, evidence-grounded target (build
  the volume→count channel), corroborated by ADR-0040-A's null and the level deficit,
  with two candidate generators recorded as refuted so they are not rediscovered.
- Positive: the located cause unifies the dispersion-sign defect and the scoring-level
  deficit under one root, so one model change is expected to move both.
- Negative: the exact within-possession empty-FGA split remains open (a bounded
  calibration item), resolvable only once the count channel exists and sim
  instrumentation is added.
- Neutral: this ADR ships no engine code; it records the direction so the model PR is
  not a re-litigation.
